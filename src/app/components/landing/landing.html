Core Capabilities

Everything Your Vehicle Deserves

From AI-powered diagnostics to precision mileage calculations — DrivePulse covers every aspect of your vehicle ownership journey.

🤖

Gemini AI Diagnostics

Real-time contextual insights from Google Gemini. Get maintenance alerts, mileage feedback, and monthly budget forecasts — with seamless rule-based fallback.

⛽

Smart Fuel Economy

Log refuels and watch DrivePulse compute precise km/L using full-tank pooling algorithms — even handling partial fills and missed fill corrections automatically.

🗺️

Trip Stopwatch Journal

Start a live riding timer, capture scenic photos, log weather conditions, and auto-score your ride quality on trip completion.

📁

Digital Document Vault

Upload insurance, PUC, and RC scans to Cloudinary. Visual countdown rings alert you of upcoming expiry dates before they sneak up on you.

💰

Expense Forecasting

Track fuel, repairs, and tax costs. DrivePulse extrapolates monthly spend using a daily run-rate model and visualises budget distributions.

🔧

Maintenance Scheduler

Monitor battery age, tyre wear, chain lubrication, and general service intervals — all degraded dynamically from your actual mileage velocity.
